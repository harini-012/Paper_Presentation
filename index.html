AI in Education

Demonstrating how AI supports students and teachers in modern learning environments

Research Overview

Artificial Intelligence is transforming education by enabling personalized learning, automated support systems, and intelligent analysis of student progress. This demo illustrates how AI tools such as learning dashboards and chatbots can assist both students and teachers in the educational process.
